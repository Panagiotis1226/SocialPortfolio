Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about working with me

What makes your approach different for family entertainment venues?

My experience in nightclub promotion taught me how to create excitement and build anticipation, but I understand that family venues require a completely different approach. I focus on creating content that highlights the joy of family time, the safety of your environment, and the memories being made. It's about building trust with parents while exciting kids about the fun they'll have.

How do you ensure content is appropriate for family audiences?

I completely understand the shift from nightlife to family-friendly content. Every piece of content goes through a family-first filter - I ask myself "Would I be comfortable sharing this with my own family?" The tone, imagery, and messaging all reflect wholesome fun, safety, and community values.

What social media platforms do you recommend for arcades?

For family entertainment venues, I typically recommend Facebook for reaching parents and building community, Instagram for visual content and younger audiences, and TikTok for fun, engaging videos that showcase the excitement of your games. Each platform serves a different purpose in building your community.

How do you measure success for social media campaigns?

Success metrics include engagement rates, reach, website traffic, and most importantly - foot traffic to your venue. I track how social media posts translate to actual visits, birthday party bookings, and repeat customers. It's not just about likes; it's about building a loyal customer base.

Can you help with special events and birthday parties?

Absolutely! Special events and birthday parties are perfect opportunities for social media content. I can create promotional materials, document the events (with parent permission), and use the content to show other families the amazing experiences they could have at your venue.

What's your typical turnaround time for flyer designs?

For standard promotional flyers, I typically deliver initial concepts within 2-3 business days, with final designs ready within a week. For rush projects or time-sensitive events, I can often accommodate faster turnarounds - just let me know your timeline and I'll work with you.
